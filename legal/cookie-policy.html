This Cookie Policy explains how Neurobard (Isha Consulting LLC) uses cookies and similar technologies on neurobard.com.

What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They serve various purposes including remembering your preferences, analyzing site usage, and enabling advertising.

Cookies We Use

Essential Cookies: Required for the Site to function properly. These include cookies that remember your cookie consent choice and theme preference (light/dark mode). These cannot be disabled.

Analytics Cookies: Help us understand how visitors interact with our Site by collecting information about pages visited, time spent, and navigation patterns. We use privacy-respecting analytics that do not personally identify visitors.

Advertising Cookies: Used by our third-party advertising partners to serve relevant ads and measure ad performance. These may track your browsing activity across different websites.

Functional Cookies: Remember choices you make (such as newsletter subscription status, cached news feed data) to provide a more personalized experience.

Third-Party Cookies

Our advertising partners and analytics providers may place cookies on your device. We do not control these cookies. The third-party services we use include advertising networks (for display ads), RSS feed aggregation services (for dynamic news content), and CDN services (for image and font delivery).

Managing Cookies

You can manage cookies through our cookie consent banner displayed when you first visit the Site, your browser settings (each browser has different cookie controls), and industry opt-out tools such as the Digital Advertising Alliance (optout.aboutads.info) and the Network Advertising Initiative (optout.networkadvertising.org).

Note that disabling certain cookies may affect the functionality of the Site.

Updates

We may update this Cookie Policy to reflect changes in technology or applicable law. Please review this page periodically.
